Spot the Difference!

Finding the three variations in objects or characters between two photographs (before and after) is the aim of each level in this game. There are three different difficulty levels: easy, medium, and hard. Each image has about ten elements or characters, but pay attention to which ones are changing! After finishing a level, move on to the next one until they are all complete.
